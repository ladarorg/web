What to expect

How the data portal will work.

When operational, this portal will provide direct access to raw sensor logs, processed atmospheric fields, and reconstructed volumetric datasets.

⬇

Direct download

Datasets available as CSV, netCDF, and JSON formats. No account required. All files include complete metadata, calibration records, and flight logs.

◈

Campaign browser

Browse data by campaign date, location, stack configuration, and sensor type. Each campaign page includes flight path visualisations and summary statistics.

⬡

Reconstructed fields

In addition to raw sensor logs, processed volumetric field reconstructions will be available with associated uncertainty estimates and reconstruction metadata.
